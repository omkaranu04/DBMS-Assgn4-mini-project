Certificate Management Information

Certificates are official documents issued to citizens for various purposes. The system supports the following certificate types:

Birth Certificate
Death Certificate
Caste Certificate
Domicile Certificate
Residence Certificate
Character Certificate

Note: All certificates require valid citizen information. Make sure the citizen is registered in the system before issuing a certificate.
