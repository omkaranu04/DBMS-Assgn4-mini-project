About Our Panchayat

Our Gram Panchayat is dedicated to the development and welfare of our village community. We work tirelessly to ensure that all citizens have access to essential services and resources.

Our mission is to:

  • Promote sustainable development
  • Ensure transparent governance
  • Facilitate citizen participation in decision-making
  • Implement welfare schemes effectively
  • Address citizen concerns promptly

We welcome your feedback and participation in making our village a better place for everyone.
